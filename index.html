About



I was born in Tehran and migrated to US in early 1980s. In addition to academics, I participated in organized sports. I have been playing soccer since I was about 5 years old. In 1980s I attended a junior college prior to attending the university for degree program. As a member of the International Student Club, I and two other students, were able to obtain permission from the state and the president of the college to start a soccer team.
